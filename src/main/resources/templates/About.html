Find-all

What do we do?

Find-all is a web application that allows users to browse multiple stores inventory to find and purchase products at the desired price point. Additional features available for customers include a covid-19 hotspot detector and a transit route finder to their desired store. Find-all aims to provide a one stop online shopping experience for its users.
